About us

Shishufoundation is a Child welfare, Non-profit Organisation established in August 13, 2020. The strength of our Organisation lies in Hard Work of its people, who come from diverse background. Shishufoundation is team of passionate and highly motivated peoples, dedicated to help every child. Our goal is to deliever food, shelter and better education to the helpless children across the nation.
